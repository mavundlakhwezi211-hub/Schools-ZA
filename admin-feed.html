About School Community Feed

Our Mission

School Community Feed is a platform designed to connect students, teachers, parents, and administrators within South African schools. Our goal is to foster communication, share updates, and build a stronger school community.

Features

• Create and share posts with your school community
• View registered learners and their profiles
• Send direct messages to teachers and other members
• Connect with your school's network
• Customize your experience with dark mode

Privacy

Your profile information is stored locally and only shared within your registered school network. We prioritize your privacy and data security.
